Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about CyberSafe Insurance.

What is cyber insurance and why does my business need it?

Cyber insurance protects your business from financial losses caused by cyber incidents such as data breaches, ransomware attacks, and other digital threats. As businesses increasingly rely on digital systems and data, the risk and potential impact of cyber attacks continues to grow. Cyber insurance helps mitigate these risks by covering costs related to incident response, business interruption, legal expenses, and more.

How much does cyber insurance cost?

The cost of cyber insurance varies depending on several factors, including your business size, industry, annual revenue, security measures in place, and the coverage limits and deductibles you choose. Small businesses might pay between $500-$5,000 annually, while larger organizations with higher risk profiles may pay more. We provide customized quotes based on your specific situation and needs.

What does CyberSafe Insurance cover?

CyberSafe Insurance provides comprehensive coverage including: data breach response costs, ransomware payments and recovery, business interruption losses, cyber extortion, digital asset restoration, security and privacy liability, regulatory defense and penalties, social engineering fraud, media liability, and 24/7 incident response services. We can customize policies to meet your organization's specific needs and risk profile.

Is my business too small to need cyber insurance?

No business is too small for cyber insurance. In fact, small and medium-sized businesses are increasingly targeted by cybercriminals because they often have fewer security resources than larger organizations. The financial impact of a cyber attack can be devastating for smaller businesses, with studies showing that up to 60% of small businesses close within six months of a major cyber incident. Cyber insurance provides critical protection regardless of your business size.

How quickly can I get coverage?

After submitting your application and completing our risk assessment process, most businesses can get coverage within 1-3 business days. For businesses with straightforward risk profiles, we can sometimes provide same-day coverage. Our streamlined application process is designed to get you protected as quickly as possible without unnecessary delays.

What support does CyberSafe provide if we experience a cyber attack?

CyberSafe provides 24/7 incident response support from the moment you detect a potential breach. Our response team includes cybersecurity experts, forensic specialists, legal advisors, and crisis management professionals who will guide you through the entire incident resolution process. We help with containment, investigation, recovery, notification, regulatory compliance, and public relations to minimize the impact on your business and reputation.
